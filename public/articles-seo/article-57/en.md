## Green Transition Jobs by Sector

### 1. Renewable Energy

**Wind Turbine Technician**: Installation, maintenance and repair of wind turbines
- Salary: $45,000 - $70,000/year
- Training: Technical degree + specialized wind training

**Solar Panel Installer**: Installation and maintenance of photovoltaic systems
- Salary: $40,000 - $60,000/year
- Training: Electrical certification + solar training

**Renewable Energy Engineer**: Design and development of renewable energy projects
- Salary: $60,000 - $100,000/year
- Training: Engineering degree specialized in energy

### 2. Building Energy Renovation

**Energy Renovation Advisor**: Supporting individuals and businesses in renovation projects
- Salary: $45,000 - $65,000/year

**Energy Auditor**: Conducting energy performance diagnostics
- Salary: $50,000 - $80,000/year

**Energy Renovation Project Manager**: Coordinating building energy renovation projects
- Salary: $65,000 - $95,000/year

### 3. Sustainable Mobility

**Electric Vehicle Technician**: Maintenance and repair of electric and hybrid vehicles
- Salary: $40,000 - $65,000/year

**Sustainable Mobility Officer**: Developing sustainable mobility solutions
- Salary: $50,000 - $80,000/year

### 4. Circular Economy

**Circular Economy Manager**: Implementing waste reduction and reuse strategies
- Salary: $60,000 - $90,000/year

**Eco-Designer**: Designing sustainable and recyclable products
- Salary: $55,000 - $85,000/year

### 5. CSR and Sustainable Development

**CSR Manager**: Defining and implementing company CSR strategy
- Salary: $70,000 - $110,000/year

**Sustainable Development Consultant**: Supporting companies in their ecological transition
- Salary: $60,000 - $100,000/year
