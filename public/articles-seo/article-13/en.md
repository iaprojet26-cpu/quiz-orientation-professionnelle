# How to Succeed in an Admission Interview: Tips and Techniques 2025

The **admission interview** is often the final step before a selective program in **Morocco**. Juries assess **motivation**, **project coherence** and **communication**. This 2025 guide covers preparation, the day itself, and follow-up.

## Preparation (2 weeks before)

- Reread your application file  
- Prepare a **2-minute introduction**  
- List **3 specific reasons** for this program  
- Take the [orientation quiz](/) if still unsure  
- Run **2 mock interviews** — use [interview prep](/tools/interview-prep)

## Common questions

Why this school? Why this career? Strengths/weaknesses with examples? Where in 5 years? Why you vs other candidates?

Use **STAR** (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for examples.

## Day-of tips

Arrive 15 minutes early, professional dress, phone off, structured answers, honest about gaps + learning plan.

## FAQ

**English required?** Depends on program — prepare a short intro if bilingual.  
**Refusal?** Analyze feedback; see [orientation guide](/blog/guide-complet-orientation-professionnelle-2025).
