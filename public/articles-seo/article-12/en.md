# Orientation Mistakes to Avoid: Complete Guide 2025

Poor orientation is expensive: lost years, stress, demotivation. In **Morocco**, family and social pressure multiply **orientation mistakes**. This guide lists common traps and how to avoid them.

## Top mistakes

1. **Salary-only choice** without daily job reality  
2. **Following parents or friends** without personal reflection — use our [quiz](/)  
3. **Ignoring the Moroccan job market** — see [Opportunities](/opportunities)  
4. **Career clichés** — meet real professionals  
5. **Last-minute decisions** — plan 6–12 months ahead ([orientation guide](/blog/guide-complet-orientation-professionnelle-2025))  
6. **Neglecting soft skills** — [essential soft skills](/blog/soft-skills-competences-professionnelles-essentielles)  
7. **Unaccredited programs** — verify diplomas on [Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco)  
8. **Underestimating English and digital skills**  
9. **No field test** before committing  
10. **Thinking orientation is fixed forever**

## Pre-decision checklist

- [ ] Quiz completed  
- [ ] 10 job ads read  
- [ ] 2 professional interviews  
- [ ] Training cost and duration known  
- [ ] Realistic plan B
