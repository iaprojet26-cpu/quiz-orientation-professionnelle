# What Career to Choose When You Don't Know What to Do? Test and Paths to Find Your Way

Are you asking yourself **"What career should I choose when I don't know what to do?"** You're not alone. This situation is very common, whether you're a student, job seeker, or considering a career change. In this complete guide, we offer **concrete paths and a free orientation test** to help you find your way.

## Why Don't You Know What Career to Choose?

### Main Causes

**1. Too Many Choices**
- The job market offers thousands of different careers
- Difficulty choosing among all options
- Fear of making the wrong choice

**2. Lack of Information**
- You don't know all the careers that exist
- You're unaware of the required skills
- You don't know what training to follow

**3. Fear of Failure**
- Fear of not succeeding in a career
- Anxiety about commitment
- Fear of wasting time

**4. Lack of Self-Knowledge**
- You don't know your strengths
- You're unaware of your real interests
- You haven't identified your professional values

## Step 3: Identify Your Transferable Skills

Even if you don't know what career to choose, you already have **skills** from your past experiences:

### Transferable Skills to Highlight

**Technical Skills**: Computer tools, foreign languages, certifications

**Soft Skills** (highly sought after):
- **Communication**: Ability to explain, convince, listen
- **Organization**: Time management, planning, prioritization
- **Teamwork**: Collaboration, coordination
- **Adaptability**: Ability to adapt to changes
- **Problem Solving**: Analysis, creativity, decision-making

Use our [**CV builder tool**](/cv) to structure your skills.

## Step 4: Ask Yourself the Right Questions

### Questions About Yourself

1. **What motivates me?** Money, recognition, autonomy, helping others, creating, solving problems?
2. **What work environment do I prefer?** Office, outdoors, home, team, alone?
3. **What are my constraints?** Geographic, financial, family, schedule?
4. **What level of stress do I accept?**
5. **What are my life goals?**
