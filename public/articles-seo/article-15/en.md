# Study or Work: How to Make the Right Decision in 2025

After high school or mid-path, many ask: **keep studying** or **enter the job market**? In **Morocco**, both paths are valid if chosen for the **right reasons**.

## When to prioritize studies

- Target job **requires** a degree  
- Clear motivation for 3–5 years  
- Funding possible (family, scholarship, apprenticeship)  
- Sector rewards diploma level strongly

Resources: [Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco), [after high school](/blog/choose-field-after-high-school-2025).

## When work may make sense

- Short **vocational** path (OFPPT, certifications) is enough  
- Internal training with employer  
- Immediate family income priority — with plan to resume studies later

## Often best: apprenticeship / vocational track

Salary + experience + diploma — bridge between “all study” and “all work.”

## Decision grid

Compare: Does job require degree? Financial situation? Motivation for school? Concrete job offer? Sector demand ([sectors hiring](/blog/secteurs-recrutent-maroc-2025))?
