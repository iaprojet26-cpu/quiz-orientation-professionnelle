# Jobs That Will Disappear by 2030: Analysis and Alternatives

**Jobs that disappear** usually mean **tasks** automated first — not entire professions overnight. By **2030**, AI and digitalization will reshape repetitive work. Plan ahead.

## Why some roles shrink

Automation, AI for tier-1 support and basic translation, digital self-service, greener industrial processes.

## Task areas under pressure

Heavy data entry, highly scripted call centers, simple repetitive production, basic bookkeeping entry.

## Human-strong areas

Care, education, complex repair, leadership, cybersecurity, advanced data, field work with uncertainty.
