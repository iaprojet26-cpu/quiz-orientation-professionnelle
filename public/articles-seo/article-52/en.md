# Top 15 Jobs Hiring Without a Degree in 2026: Opportunities and Training

Looking for a **job that hires without a degree**? Good news: many sectors hire without requiring initial qualifications. In this complete guide, we present **15 accessible jobs** with short training, attractive salaries, and real opportunities for advancement.

## Why Do Some Jobs Hire Without a Degree?

### Main Reasons

**1. Labor Shortage**: Some sectors lack qualified candidates
**2. Practical Skills Priority**: Experience and practical skills matter more than diplomas
**3. Evolving Recruitment Criteria**: Employers increasingly value soft skills
**4. Fast-Growing Sectors**: Massive need for personnel in certain sectors

## Top 15 Jobs Hiring Without a Degree in 2026

### 1. Delivery Driver
- **Training**: Driver's license, few days internal training
- **Salary**: $1,800 - $2,500/month
- **Growth**: Logistics manager, team leader

### 2. Security Guard
- **Training**: Security certification (3 weeks)
- **Salary**: $1,700 - $2,200/month
- **Growth**: Security supervisor, security manager

### 3. Order Picker / Warehouse Worker
- **Training**: Internal training (1-2 weeks), forklift certification
- **Salary**: $1,800 - $2,200/month
- **Growth**: Team leader, logistics manager

### 4. Server / Waiter
- **Training**: On-the-job training
- **Salary**: Minimum wage + tips (can reach $2,200-2,800/month)
- **Growth**: Head waiter, restaurant manager

### 5. Cleaning Staff
- **Training**: Internal training (few days)
- **Salary**: $1,700 - $2,000/month
- **Growth**: Team supervisor, cleaning business owner

### 6. Call Center Agent
- **Training**: Internal training (2-4 weeks)
- **Salary**: $1,700 - $2,200/month (+ bonuses)
- **Growth**: Supervisor, trainer, quality manager

### 7. Warehouse Handler
- **Training**: Internal training, forklift certification
- **Salary**: $1,800 - $2,200/month
- **Growth**: Team leader, stock manager

### 8. Retail Sales Associate
- **Training**: Internal training (1-2 weeks)
- **Salary**: $1,700 - $2,200/month (+ commissions)
- **Growth**: Department manager, store manager

### 9. Home Care Assistant
- **Training**: Short professional training (3-6 months)
- **Salary**: $1,700 - $2,000/month
- **Growth**: Team coordinator, trainer

### 10. Construction Worker
- **Training**: On-site training
- **Salary**: $1,800 - $2,400/month
- **Growth**: Skilled worker, team leader, site manager

### 11. Kitchen Helper / Cook
- **Training**: Internal training or culinary apprenticeship
- **Salary**: $1,800 - $2,400/month
- **Growth**: Line cook, sous chef, head chef

### 12. Activity Leader / Animator
- **Training**: Animation certification (2 weeks + internship)
- **Salary**: Variable (seasonal), ~$1,700-2,200/month
- **Growth**: Center director, youth coordinator

### 13. Production Operator
- **Training**: Internal training (1-2 weeks)
- **Salary**: $1,800 - $2,200/month
- **Growth**: Team leader, technician

### 14. Receptionist
- **Training**: Internal training (few days to 1 week)
- **Salary**: $1,700 - $2,100/month
- **Growth**: Front desk manager, secretary

### 15. Heavy Equipment Operator
- **Training**: Equipment certification (few days to 1 week)
- **Salary**: $2,000 - $2,800/month
- **Growth**: Team leader, site supervisor
