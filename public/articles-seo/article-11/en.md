# How to Choose Your Field After High School: Complete Orientation Guide 2025

Graduation opens a door — but you must choose the **right direction**. In **Morocco**, between public universities, private schools, OFPPT and competitive exams, options are numerous. This guide helps you pick a **coherent field** aligned with your abilities, goals and the **job market**.

## Why this choice matters

A misaligned field can mean costly **reorientation**, repeated academic struggle, and slower job insertion. A thoughtful choice supports internships, networks and motivation over 3–5 years.

## 5 pillars of post-high school orientation

### 1. Know your profile (not only grades)

Grades matter for admission, but your **professional profile** guides the field. Take our [free orientation quiz](/).

### 2. Balance passion, project and market

| Question | Goal |
|----------|------|
| What motivates me long term? | Sustainable motivation |
| What job do I target in 5 years? | Concrete project |
| Does this sector hire in Morocco? | Employability |

### 3. Explore training paths

Universities, engineering and business schools, **OFPPT**, specialized schools. See [Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco).

### 4. Research real outcomes

Read job ads; cross-check [future careers](/blog/metiers-davenir-2025-2030) and [employability](/blog/employabilite-marche-travail-2025).

### 5. Validate on the ground

Internships, summer jobs, professional interviews — **one day in the field** beats weeks of theory.

## In-demand fields in Morocco (2025)

Engineering & IT, business & finance, health, law & humanities (with specialization), tourism & hospitality, energy & environment.

## Common mistakes

Choosing for parents only, following friends blindly, ignoring prerequisites, underestimating total cost, neglecting English.

## 4-week method

**Week 1** — quiz + 5 target jobs  
**Week 2** — compare programs on [Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco)  
**Week 3** — two interviews (student + professional)  
**Week 4** — written comparison + plan A / plan B

## FAQ

**Change field after one year?** Possible but costly; better to orient well early.  
**Sciences required for digital?** Check each school’s requirements.
