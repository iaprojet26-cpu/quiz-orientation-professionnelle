# Top 10 Soft Skills in Demand in Companies in 2025

Moroccan employers — SMEs, multinationals, BPO, startups — often choose candidates on **soft skills** when technical profiles look alike.

## The top 10

1. Communication  
2. Teamwork  
3. Adaptability  
4. Autonomy  
5. Problem solving  
6. Time management  
7. Emotional intelligence  
8. Critical thinking  
9. Leadership  
10. Reliability and ethics

Full guide: [essential soft skills](/blog/soft-skills-competences-professionnelles-essentielles).

## Prove them on your CV

Use **action + context + result**, not empty lists like “dynamic, motivated.”

Tools: [CV](/cv) · [ATS](/tools/ats-score).

## FAQ

**Innate or learned?** Mostly learned through practice.  
**First job priority?** Communication, reliability, learning speed.
