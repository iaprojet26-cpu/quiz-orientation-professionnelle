# How to Boost Your Employability in 2025: Effective Strategies

**Employability** is your ability to win jobs, internships or promotions — not just holding a diploma.

1. Clear project — [quiz](/), [define your project](/blog/define-professional-project-a-z)  
2. Optimized [CV](/blog/write-perfect-cv-templates-2025) and [cover letter](/blog/cover-letter-guide-examples-2025)  
3. [Digital](/blog/essential-digital-skills-graduates-2025) and [soft skills](/blog/soft-skills-competences-professionnelles-essentielles)  
4. Experience (internships, projects)  
5. Network — [Opportunities](/opportunities)  
6. Sector watch — [hiring sectors](/blog/secteurs-recrutent-maroc-2025)

## 90-day plan

Month 1 — skills audit, CV refresh  
Month 2 — certification or portfolio, 10 targeted applications  
Month 3 — interviews and iteration
