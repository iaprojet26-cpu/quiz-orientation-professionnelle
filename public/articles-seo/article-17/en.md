# Essential Digital Skills for Young Graduates in 2025

**Digital transformation** affects every sector in **Morocco**. For **young graduates**, digital basics are an **employability prerequisite**, not only for IT roles.

## Level 1 — Advanced office

Excel (formulas, pivots), Word, PowerPoint/Canva, professional PDFs.

## Level 2 — Collaboration

Google Workspace or Microsoft 365, video calls, task tools (Trello, Notion basics).

## Level 3 — Professional online presence

Complete **LinkedIn**, professional email, mindful e-reputation.

## Level 4 — Data & automation (differentiator)

CSV, SQL basics, Power Query, critical use of **generative AI**.

See [future careers](/blog/metiers-davenir-2025-2030).

## Level 5 — Cybersecurity basics

Strong passwords, 2FA, no client data on personal apps, phishing awareness.

## 3-month learning plan

Month 1 — Excel + presentations  
Month 2 — collaboration + LinkedIn  
Month 3 — SQL or sector tool + certification if possible
