# Jobs That Will Explode Thanks to AI in 2025-2030

**Artificial intelligence** creates demand for people who **integrate, secure, and govern** AI — not only coders.

## Growing roles

- Data analyst / data scientist  
- ML engineer (after solid foundations)  
- Data engineer  
- Cybersecurity specialist  
- Digital transformation consultant  
- Prompt / generative AI specialist (hybrid profiles)  
- Digital product owner  
- Full-stack / cloud developers

## Core skills

Statistics, critical thinking on AI outputs, ethics, communication, technical English.
