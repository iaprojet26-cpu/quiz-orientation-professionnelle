# Data Analyst Career: Training, Salary, Skills and Opportunities in 2025

A **data analyst** turns numbers into **business decisions**. In **Morocco**, banks, telecom, e-commerce and startups hire profiles skilled in SQL, advanced Excel and dashboards.

## Role

Collect and clean data; build dashboards (Power BI, Tableau); answer business questions; present insights clearly.

## Key skills

Excel, **SQL**, basic statistics, Python/R optional, visualization. Communication and rigor — [soft skills](/blog/top-10-soft-skills-companies-2025).

## Training

University (math, stats, business), engineering schools, bootcamps/MOOCs, **portfolio projects**.

[Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco)

## Indicative salaries (Morocco, 2025)

- Junior: ~6,000–10,000 MAD/month  
- Mid: 10,000–16,000 MAD/month  
- Senior: 16,000–25,000+ MAD/month  

## Hiring sectors

Finance, telecom, retail, logistics, healthcare data, public digital projects.
