# CV and Orientation: How to Align Your CV with Your Profile

Creating an effective CV isn't just about listing your skills and experiences. A truly high-performing CV is one that's aligned with your professional orientation profile. Discover how to identify your profile and adapt your CV accordingly to maximize your chances of landing your ideal job.

## Why Align Your CV with Your Orientation Profile?

### Understanding Your Professional Profile

Your professional orientation profile reflects your personality, values, motivations, and natural skills. There are generally 5 main profiles:

1. **Creative Profile**: Innovation, imagination, artistic expression
2. **Technical Profile**: Logic, problem solving, precision
3. **Social Profile**: Communication, empathy, service to others
4. **Organizational Profile**: Management, planning, leadership
5. **Entrepreneurial Profile**: Vision, risk taking, autonomy

### Benefits of an Aligned CV

A CV aligned with your orientation profile allows you to:

- **Be Authentic**: Present your true professional personality
- **Maximize Your Assets**: Highlight your natural skills
- **Find the Right Job**: Target jobs that match your profile
- **Stand Out**: Create a coherent and memorable CV
- **Increase Your Satisfaction**: Work in an environment that suits you

## Aligning Your CV According to Your Profile

### Creative Profile

**Characteristics:** Innovation, imagination, artistic expression, divergent thinking

**How to Adapt Your CV:**

- **Professional Objective**: Highlight your creativity and ability to innovate
- **Skills**: Design, Creativity, Innovation, Arts, Visual Communication
- **Experiences**: Creative projects, portfolios, artistic achievements
- **Interests**: Arts, design, music, creative writing

**Example Objective:**
> "Creative designer passionate about innovation and visual expression. 3 years of experience in graphic design and UI/UX with a portfolio of 20+ creative projects. Seeking a creative designer position to bring an innovative vision and create memorable visual experiences."

**Adapted Jobs:** Designer, Graphic Designer, Creative Writer, Architect, Photographer

### Technical Profile

**Characteristics:** Logic, precision, problem solving, analysis

**How to Adapt Your CV:**

- **Professional Objective**: Emphasize your logic and ability to solve complex problems
- **Skills**: Programming, Analysis, Mathematics, Engineering, Problem Solving
- **Experiences**: Technical projects, certifications, technical achievements
- **Interests**: Technologies, sciences, puzzles, DIY projects

**Example Objective:**
> "Computer engineer specialized in backend development with 4 years of experience. Expertise in solving complex problems and performance optimization. Mastery of Python, Java, and microservices architectures. Seeking a senior engineer position to contribute to ambitious technical projects."

**Adapted Jobs:** Developer, Engineer, Data Analyst, System Architect, Researcher

### Social Profile

**Characteristics:** Communication, empathy, service to others, teamwork

**How to Adapt Your CV:**

- **Professional Objective**: Value your communication and ability to help others
- **Skills**: Communication, Empathy, Customer Service, Team Management, Training
- **Experiences**: Volunteering, associations, customer service, teaching
- **Interests**: Volunteering, team sports, social activities

**Example Objective:**
> "Human resources professional with 5 years of experience in recruitment and talent development. Passionate about supporting people and creating positive work environments. Expertise in interpersonal communication and career management. Seeking an HR manager position to contribute to team well-being and growth."

**Adapted Jobs:** HR, Teacher, Doctor, Psychologist, Coach, Salesperson

### Organizational Profile

**Characteristics:** Management, planning, leadership, organization

**How to Adapt Your CV:**

- **Professional Objective**: Highlight your ability to manage and organize
- **Skills**: Project Management, Leadership, Planning, Organization, Management
- **Experiences**: Team management, event organization, structured projects
- **Interests**: Management, organization, strategy, leadership

**Example Objective:**
> "Project Manager with 6 years of experience managing IT projects and multidisciplinary teams. Expertise in Agile methodologies and strategic planning. Track record of 15+ projects delivered on time and budget. Seeking a senior project manager position to lead strategic projects and develop team skills."

**Adapted Jobs:** Project Manager, Manager, Director, Consultant, Business Analyst

### Entrepreneurial Profile

**Characteristics:** Vision, risk taking, autonomy, innovation

**How to Adapt Your CV:**

- **Professional Objective**: Show your vision and ability to take initiative
- **Skills**: Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Risk Taking, Strategic Vision, Autonomy
- **Experiences**: Startups, entrepreneurial projects, personal initiatives
- **Interests**: Entrepreneurship, innovation, business, investment

**Example Objective:**
> "Entrepreneur and innovation consultant with 7 years of experience launching and developing tech startups. Expertise in business strategy, fundraising, and scaling. Founder of 2 startups with successful exit. Seeking an innovation director position to develop new products and explore new markets."

**Adapted Jobs:** Entrepreneur, Consultant, Sales Director, Business Developer, Investor

## Tips to Maintain Alignment

### 1. Stay Authentic

Don't force your profile. If you're naturally creative, don't pretend to be technical just for a position.

### 2. Evolve with Your Profile

Your profile can evolve with experience. Reassess it regularly.

### 3. Adapt Without Betraying

You can adapt your CV to a posting while remaining faithful to your profile.
