# Complete career orientation guide 2025

Orientation is not picking a degree at random: it is building a **coherent path** between who you are, what you can do, and what the **job market** needs. This guide is for high school students, graduates, and career changers in **Morocco**.

## Four pillars of good orientation

1. **Know yourself** — [free quiz](/), interests, constraints  
2. **Know careers** — [career paths](/career-paths), interviews with professionals  
3. **Know training** — [Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco)  
4. **Know the market** — [Opportunities](/opportunities), [employability](/blog/employabilite-marche-travail-2025)

## 6-step method

1. Take the quiz and note 5 appealing jobs  
2. Shortlist 3 careers to research  
3. Compare 2 programs per career  
4. Plan immersion (internship, shadowing)  
5. Decide plan A and plan B  
6. Prepare insertion: CV, [career guides](/career-guides)
