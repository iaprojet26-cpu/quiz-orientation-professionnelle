# Career change: complete guide to switching jobs

Changing careers is increasingly common in **Morocco**: digital transformation, search for meaning, or sector shifts. This guide walks you through a realistic **career change** from reflection to job search.

## 7 steps to a successful change

1. **Skills audit** — transferable skills, constraints, deal-breakers  
2. **Explore 2–3 target jobs** — [career paths](/career-paths), [future jobs](/blog/metiers-davenir-2025-2030)  
3. **Gap analysis** — training, certifications, portfolio  
4. **Choose training** — university, OFPPT, bootcamps ([Study in Morocco](/study-in-morocco))  
5. **Plan budget and timeline** (often 6–18 months)  
6. **Transition CV** — [CV guide](/blog/rediger-cv-parfait-modeles-2025)  
7. **Clear narrative in interviews**

## Accessible sectors in Morocco

| Sector | Notes |
|--------|--------|
| Digital / data | Certifications, strong demand |
| Sales / customer service | Transferable experience |
| HR / training | Communication profiles |
| Tourism / events | Languages, service mindset |

## FAQ

**Age limit?** No fixed limit; coherence and recent proof matter.  
**Keep current job?** Evening training or part-time paths when possible.
